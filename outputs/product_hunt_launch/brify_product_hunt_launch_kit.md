## FAQ

### Is Brify just another AI summarizer?

Not exactly. Brify can summarize, but its main focus is structure. It separates long material into an editable map of questions, claims, evidence, limitations, and next steps.

### Who is Brify for?

Students, researchers, graduate students, founders, analysts, and anyone who needs to read and reuse long documents.

### Can I use it for research papers?

Yes. Brify is designed to help you organize papers by research question, method, results, evidence, and limitations. You should still verify important points against the original source.

### Does Brify replace reading?

No. Brify is a review and structuring tool. It helps you understand where to focus and what to check, but important documents still need source verification.

### What makes it different from ChatGPT or NotebookLM?

ChatGPT is flexible, and NotebookLM is useful for source-grounded exploration. Brify focuses on turning dense material into editable structure maps that are easier to review, compare, and reuse.
